Basic Concepts

Every piece of data has a story. Maybe it's about how many pizzas were sold last month or how many rainbows appeared this year. With Tableau, we can share these stories in a fun and exciting way. Let's be storytellers!

Have you ever seen a colorful chart or graph in a presentation? That's data visualization! It's a fun way to show information. Instead of looking at boring numbers, we can see pictures and colors to understand data better. It's like turning math into art!
