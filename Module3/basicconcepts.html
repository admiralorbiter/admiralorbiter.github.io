Before we can create cool charts, we need some data! Tableau can use data from many places, like Excel or the internet. Let's learn how to give Tableau the information it needs to make our awesome visuals.
In Tableau, there are three main parts: Sheets, Dashboards, and Stories. Sheets are where we build our charts. Dashboards are like a collage of many charts. And Stories? They're a fun way to share our charts in a slideshow. Cool, right?
