PREP-KC is an organization dedicated to enhancing education and empowering students.

8th Grade Data in Action Course

Data in Action! blends exploring the data science process with experiential learning. The more a student can see the relevance in learning as well as seeing themselves having a possible future in a career only strengthens their beliefs of achieving their dreams. Each module for Data in Action! enhances the exploration of the data science process by providing students the opportunity to interact with Industry through various means.
