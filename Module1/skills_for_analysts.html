Skills for the Modern Data Analyst

A proficient data analyst needs a mix of both hard and soft skills.

  • Business Concepts and Technology: Understanding the industry you're analyzing is crucial. Technology, especially various software tools, is fundamental in processing large datasets.
  • Presentation Skills: An analyst must be able to communicate their findings clearly and effectively.
  • Critical Thinking and Visualization: Interpreting the data correctly and representing it in a way that's understandable is key.
  • Math and Statistics: The foundation of data analysis lies in math and statistics.
